Best Friends

Over a pint of cold dark beer, a rough gang challenges you and your friends to several odd competitions. The locals say that they’re the best in the world and that you’re barking mad to take them on, but they haven’t seen what your friends can unleash.

Challenge 1: Who can make it furthest with this revamped ironing board?

Challenge 2: Which ballboy can stay ready, in this match between servers who always fault?

Challenge 3: Who will get the peanut butter jar the cleanest?

Challenge 4: Who will be faster in Total Wipeout Sweepout?

Challenge 5: Who will win at reaching the cookie jar?

Challenge 6: Who can recover this diamond ring from the ocean floor?

Best Friends

Over a pint of cold dark beer, a rough gang challenges you and your friends to several odd competitions. The locals say that they’re the best in the world and that you’re barking mad to take them on, but they haven’t seen what your friends can unleash.

Challenge 1: Furthest distance on a floating ironing board

Challenge 2: Ballboy, but when the servers always fault

Challenge 3: Get the cleanest peanut butter jar

Challenge 4: Total Wipeout Sweepout

Challenge 5: Reach the cookie jar

Challenge 6: Find the sunken pirate ship
